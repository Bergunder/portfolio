Me

Hello, my name is Jason Bergunder.

I have been a hobby enthusiast of miniature model painting for 9 years and owned StaticGrass Creations for 5 years. I have taken a number of commissions over the years and have started to sell models on Ebay as well. Over the past 4 years, I have been studying media arts and design at Simon Fraser University in the School of Interactive Arts and Technology (SIAT).

I love what I do.

9 Years

My hobby includes miniature model building and painting for table-top games such as Warhammer and Warmachine, but I would love to paint any kind of miniature model. I am starting to take a look at Malifaux and Kingdom Death models. I also create video tutorials on the model making process and the advanced techiques used to paint them.

4 Years

My time at SFU has shown me how to work with people of different skillsets and backgrounds. As well as conveying my ideas to people that do not have the same vocabulary as I do. I have learned the fundamentals of design for both print and for the web.
